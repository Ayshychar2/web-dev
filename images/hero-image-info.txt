For the hero image, you should use a high-quality image that represents web development or digital services.

Recommended specifications:
- Resolution: At least 1920x1080 pixels
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Style: Dark-themed to match the website's aesthetic
- Content: Could show a developer working, code on screen, or a modern digital interface

You can find suitable images on:
- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
- Pexels (https://pexels.com)
- Pixabay (https://pixabay.com)

Search terms like "web development", "coding", "dark theme developer", or "digital technology" should yield good results.

Remember to optimize the image for web to ensure fast loading times.
